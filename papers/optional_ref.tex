\chapter{Motivation}

Optionals holding references are common other than in the standard libary's implementation. The research in \cite{P1683} shows conclusively that rebind semantics are the only safe semantic as assign through on engaged is too bug-prone. Implementations that attempt assign-through are abandoned. The standard library should follow existing practice and supply an optional<T&> that rebinds on assignment.
